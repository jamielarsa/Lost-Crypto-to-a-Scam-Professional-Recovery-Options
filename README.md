A Realistic Example

Imagine an investor deposits $50,000 into what appears to be a legitimate crypto investment platform.

For several weeks, the dashboard shows increasing profits and successful trades. When the investor attempts to withdraw funds, additional fees are requested. After paying those fees, communication suddenly stops and access to the account is restricted.

A blockchain forensic review may reveal that the funds were transferred through multiple intermediary wallets before reaching other destinations. While no ethical investigator can guarantee recovery, tracing those movements can help establish a clearer picture of the fraud and identify potential investigative opportunities.

Protect Yourself From Recovery Scams

Unfortunately, many victims become targets for a second scam after reporting their losses online.

Be cautious of anyone who:

Guarantees recovery
Requests your seed phrase
Claims immediate access to stolen funds
Demands large upfront payments without explaining their process

Legitimate recovery professionals focus on evidence, transparency, and realistic expectations. Any service claiming guaranteed results should be approached with extreme caution.
